# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Product Overview
AutoStream is a SaaS platform for content creators that automates video editing workflows so teams can create polished content faster with less manual effort.

## Basic Plan
The Basic plan costs $29/month. It includes 10 videos per month and exports up to 720p resolution.

## Pro Plan
The Pro plan costs $79/month. It includes unlimited videos, 4K resolution exports, and AI captions. Pro users also get access to premium support coverage.

## Company Policies
AutoStream does not offer refunds after 7 days. 24/7 support is available only on the Pro plan.
